Cookie Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Last Updated: July 26, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, provide a better user experience, and provide information to the owners of the site.

DeshChain uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to distinguish you from other users of our platform. This helps us provide you with a good experience when you browse our platform and allows us to improve our services.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the platform to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility.

  • Session Management: Maintain your session as you navigate the platform
  • Security: Help detect and prevent security threats
  • Load Balancing: Ensure optimal performance by distributing traffic
  • User Preferences: Remember your settings like language and theme

2.2 Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how you use our platform, helping us improve its performance.

  • Analytics: Track page views, traffic sources, and user interactions
  • Error Reporting: Help identify and fix technical issues
  • Performance Monitoring: Measure loading times and response rates

2.3 Functionality Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization.

  • Language Preferences: Remember your preferred language (including 22 Indian languages)
  • Theme Settings: Store your choice of light/dark mode or cultural themes
  • Wallet Connection: Remember your connected wallet preferences
  • Tutorial Progress: Track which guides you've completed

2.4 Marketing Cookies

We use limited marketing cookies to understand the effectiveness of our community outreach.

  • Campaign Tracking: Measure the success of our educational campaigns
  • Community Analytics: Understand how users find and join our community

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our platform:

  • Google Analytics: Used to understand how visitors engage with our platform
  • CloudFlare: Used for security and performance optimization
  • GitHub: Used when embedding code examples or repository information

5. Local Storage and Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we use similar technologies:

  • Local Storage: Stores larger amounts of data locally on your device
  • Session Storage: Temporary storage that's cleared when you close your browser
  • IndexedDB: Used for storing transaction history and cached blockchain data

6. Managing Cookies

6.1 Cookie Consent

When you first visit our platform, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Decline non-essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences

6.2 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Privacy, search, and services > Cookies and site permissions

6.3 Opt-Out Links

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-Out
  • Your Online Choices: EU Cookie Preferences

7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies, please note that:

  • Some features of the platform may not function properly
  • You may need to re-enter preferences each time you visit
  • Performance may be impacted as we cannot optimize based on usage patterns
  • Security features may be less effective

8. Cookies and Blockchain

Important: Cookies are separate from blockchain data:

  • Cookies are stored on your device and can be deleted
  • Blockchain transactions are permanent and public
  • Deleting cookies does not affect your blockchain transaction history
  • Your wallet address and transactions remain visible on the blockchain regardless of cookie settings

9. Updates to Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Displaying a notice on our platform
  • Requesting renewed consent where required
